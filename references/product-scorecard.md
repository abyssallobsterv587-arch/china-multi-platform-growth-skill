# Product Scorecard

Use this scorecard before investing time in a campaign.

Score each category from 1 to 5.

## Scoring Dimensions

### 1. Visual Demonstrability

- Can the product be shown in 3 to 10 seconds?
- Is there an obvious before/after?
- Does the result read clearly on mobile?

### 2. Pain Clarity

- Is the user problem easy to describe in one sentence?
- Will the buyer recognize the pain immediately?

### 3. Purchase Simplicity

- Is the offer easy to understand?
- Can the user decide quickly without long explanation?

### 4. Platform Fit

- Does the product naturally match the chosen platform?
- Can it be sold with the platform's content style?

### 5. Margin / Monetization Headroom

- Is there room for paid traffic, discounts, or creator cost?
- Is the price high enough to be worth selling?

### 6. Trust Burden

- Does the product need low trust to convert?
- Or does it require heavy proof, reviews, and education?

Low trust burden = higher score.

### 7. Compliance Risk

- Are claims easy to keep compliant?
- Is it far from medical, financial, legal, or restricted categories?

Low compliance risk = higher score.

## Interpretation

- 28 to 35: Strong test candidate
- 21 to 27: Testable with stronger angle work
- 14 to 20: Weak candidate, use only if strategically necessary
- Below 14: Skip

## Output Format

Return:
- Product
- Total score
- Top strengths
- Main risks
- Test recommendation

## Quick Rule

If the product scores low on:
- Visual demonstrability
- Pain clarity
- Platform fit

Do not launch content yet. Reposition or pick another product.
