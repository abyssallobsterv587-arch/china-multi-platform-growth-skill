# Platform Playbook

## Douyin

Best for:
- Visual demos
- Fast hooks
- Direct response offers

Content format:
- First 2 seconds: pain point or result
- Mid: proof, demo, contrast
- End: clear CTA

Good asset types:
- Hook line
- 15s script
- Caption
- CTA variants

## Xiaohongshu

Best for:
- Searchable discovery
- Lifestyle framing
- Trust-building notes

Content format:
- Search-friendly title
- Experience-led body
- Comment bait and save-worthy structure

Good asset types:
- Note title
- Cover text
- Body outline
- FAQ comments

## Kuaishou

Best for:
- Mass-market utility
- Straight selling
- Repetition and familiarity

Content format:
- Direct opening
- Practical demo
- Low-friction CTA

## WeChat / Chat

Best for:
- Follow-up
- Warm lead conversion
- Service and retention

Use:
- Short, human replies
- One CTA per message
- No mass unsolicited outreach

## Ecommerce Platforms

For Taobao / JD / Pinduoduo / 1688 style listings:
- Prioritize title clarity over cleverness
- Lead with buyer intent keywords
- Use 3 to 5 benefit bullets
- Prepare objection-handling answers

Always create:
- Product title
- Short description
- Long description
- FAQ
- Risk notes

## Selection Heuristics

Prefer offers with:
- Fast visual proof
- Obvious use case
- Clear buyer pain
- Simple pricing
- Low explanation burden

Avoid:
- High compliance products
- Products requiring unsupported claims
- Offers that depend on fake social proof
